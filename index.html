Why 3D Artist ?

First, because it's the era of the CGI effect, now is the era where advanced technology is rampant, especially the CGI effect technology. Everywhere we have seen this CGI effect, especially in the world of cinema and video games. CGI effects are also part of the expertise of a 3D Artist, such as creating 3D models, creating animations, creating visual effects, and much more.

Second, it doesn't cost a lot to prepare a 3D animation/model that is suitable for a particular purpose. Even investing in a learning animation/3D model will pay off. This method is considered cheaper than other communication media on the market today. The benefits that will be obtained are far greater than the money invested in learning a 3D animation/model.

Last, many look at it and can also increase company growth and business image, especially companies that need it for marketing. Because if you have an 3D animated version of a product or service, it can be applied to various media which leads to massive publications online. This way, your business will be affordable by millions of consumers, which will increase business growth opportunities.

Actually there are many advantages that by being / having a 3D Artist especially in a company both large and small, it will help a company that the company is technology literate. Thanks for reading, best regards CGI :)
